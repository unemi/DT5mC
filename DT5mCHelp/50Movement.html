You can modify the movement of swarm by the following four parameters:

  1. Speed: velocity magnitude of agents in target area.
  2. Turn: turning angle.
  3. Avoidance: weight of repellent force for integration with attraction force.
  4. Hi speed th.: upper threshold of attractant's density for agent into high speed exploration.
